# Alderstone Consulting Website Brief

## Project Overview

Create a professional, production ready website for **Alderstone Consulting**, a data consulting firm founded by two experienced data experts, **Seun Kasali** and **Komi Adenosine**.

Alderstone Consulting helps SMEs and mid sized companies leverage their data to improve decision making, automate reporting, and build scalable data infrastructure.

The website should look professional, trustworthy, and consulting focused, similar in style to modern consulting firms and boutique data consultancies. Avoid startup style hype language.

The design must be clean, minimal, corporate, and credibility focused.

---

## Target Audience

The website should speak clearly to:

- Small and medium sized businesses
- Scaleups
- E commerce companies
- SaaS companies
- Businesses with growing data needs but no internal data team

---

## Website Goals

The website should:

- Build trust
- Clearly explain services
- Generate consultation calls
- Position Alderstone Consulting as experts in data infrastructure and analytics

---

## Brand Tone

The tone of the website should be:

- Professional
- Clear
- Business focused
- Free from buzzwords or hype
- Focused on business outcomes

---

## Website Structure

Create the following pages:

- Home
- Services
- Industries
- Case Studies
- About
- Insights / Blog
- Contact

Include a header navigation and footer on every page.

---

## Design Guidelines

### Style

- Clean consulting style
- White background
- Neutral color palette
- Professional typography
- Clear hierarchy
- Generous spacing
- Subtle animations

### Color Palette

- **Primary color:** Deep blue or navy
- **Secondary color:** Slate gray
- **Accent color:** Light blue

### Typography

Use a modern professional sans serif font such as:

- Inter
- Helvetica
- or a similar alternative

### Visual Direction

Include icons and subtle graphics representing:

- Data pipelines
- Analytics dashboards
- Infrastructure
- Business insights

Avoid stock photos that look overly corporate.
